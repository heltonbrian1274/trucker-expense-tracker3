❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?+

Absolutely. All data is stored on your device only. We have no servers and cannot see your information.

How do I backup my data?+

On the main screen, click the 'Backup' button. This will save a JSON file of your data to your device. Keep it in a safe place.

What happens after the trial?+

After the 30-day free trial, you'll need to subscribe to continue adding new expenses. Your existing data will remain accessible.
